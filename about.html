Welcome to Health Plus

We are a group of passionate and energetic healthcare professions that provide not just better healthcare, but a better healthcare experience. We are unique in a way that we want to create a health movement within individual, expanding to community scale. It is our desire to transform healthcare in every direction and bring the future of healthcare to you.

We appreciate the opportunity that you have given us to take care of you and your family. As your local health service, we will strive to provide you with highest standard of care. Your health and wellbeing is our number one concern. Your health goals are our goals!

Thank you for choosing us as your local health service and we look forward to serving you.

Our Vision

We believe the heart of healthcare is service to others.

We recognise each patient’s value and their needs and have a holistic approach in everything we do. Your personal wellbeing, your family, and our community are the most important things that matter to us.

At health plus. we are guided by 4 simple principles:

  • Passion – we love everything we do, we love to see you achieving better health, most of all, we love our community
  • Lifestyle – there is no better way achieving a good health, via promoting and educating the benefit of healthy and active lifestyles, when you look after your body, then your body will look after you
  • Understanding – we understand that every patient has different need, and we certainly will customize our care to suit you.
  • Support – life is a long journey; you should never be alone. Let us strive to be your healthcare partner for life
